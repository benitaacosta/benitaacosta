About

Welcome! My name is Benita Acosta. I am a print production artist with over 6 years experience specializing in vector graphics and wide format printing. I have over 4 years of Digital Media education where I have gained the technical and design skills in photo editing, web design, and pre-press design, all on a variety of platforms including the latest Adobe Creative Cloud programs such as Photoshop and Illustrator. When I am not creating, I enjoy spending time with my family and seeing everything San Antonio has to offer. Although I have lived here all my life, I still haven't seen it all. The art and the people who surround me in this city are incredibly inspiring and I hope that some day, I can contribute something to it.
